About Our School

Nurturing Potential,
Building Character

At St. Mary's Academy, we believe every child has unique potential waiting to be unlocked. Our holistic approach combines rigorous academics with character development, ensuring students graduate not just with knowledge, but with the values and skills to make a positive impact on Nigeria and the world.

Academic Excellence

Comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge and inspire every learner.

Character Development

Building integrity, empathy, and leadership skills for life success.

Community Focus

Inclusive environment where every student belongs and thrives.

Academic Programs

Comprehensive Education Pathways

From early childhood through high school, we offer programs that cater to every stage of your child's educational journey.

Primary School

Grades 1-5: Building strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, and social skills through play-based and structured learning.

  • Phonics & Reading
  • STEM Introduction
  • Arts & Music

Middle School

Grades 6-8: Transitioning to independent learning with specialized subjects and extracurricular exploration.

  • Advanced Mathematics
  • Laboratory Sciences
  • Digital Literacy

High School

Grades 9-12: College preparatory curriculum with AP courses, career counseling, and leadership opportunities.

  • AP & Honors Courses
  • College Counseling
  • Internship Programs
